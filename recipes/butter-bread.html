Butter Bread

Description

Butter bread is such a simple dish, yet a very flavorful one. The bread is super fluffy and the buttery taste just makes it a very fulfilling dish.

  • Hawaiian Rolls
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Open up the rolls with your hands to create a separation.
  2. In that separation, spread the butter around.
  3. Depending on the strength of your microwave, it should be 15-30 seconds.
